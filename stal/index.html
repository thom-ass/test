We Care to Deliver

STAL has always put occupational health and safety procedure for every single worker in operational team as top priority without any exception to avoid any injury and accident that might happen during the operation process.

Journey Management Program

STAL provides real-time tracking and live-monitoring for every journey with the latest cutting edge technology including GPS Monitoring and In-Cabin Camera embedded inside the armadas, ensuring all the goods to be delivered safely and properly.

Driver Management Program

Every STAL’s driver has already passed the selection and trainining process as a requirement to join the logistic team, so they’re able to serve and work even more resourceful than others. Every driver will be accompanied by 1 co-driver on every logistic/delivery task.

Emergency Response Team

Commited to provide responsive and hassle-free logistic service for its respective customers, STAL provides non-stop emergency response team including car and operational team for 24 hours a day, and 7-Days in a week to handle any problems or mitigate potential hazards that might occur on the road for every ongoing shipment from pickup to recipient’s place.
