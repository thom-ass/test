OUR MILESTONES
1971

Mr. Gunawan Suteja established Po Sinar Jaya, a trucking company located in Mojokerto, East Java.

1981

Opened its first representatives in Jakarta and changed its name to PT. Sinar Transindomitra Abadi.

1997-2004

In 1997 First colaborated with PT. AMI as one of the transporter (now PT.BPPI)
In 2004 PT. STA Logistik (a new company) was established as we were appointed by PT. AMI to be its sole transported and warehousing contractor.

2010

Along with the needs for business expansion and business opportunities (bulk deliver), PT. Sinar Transindomitra Abadi changed its name to PT. Multisukses Mitra Sejati.
